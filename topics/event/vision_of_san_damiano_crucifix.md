---
description: "The Vision of the San Damiano Crucifix"
---

While praying in the ruined church of San Damiano, Francis heard the crucifix speak to him: ‘Francis, go and repair my house, which you see is falling into ruin.’ He first took the words literally and rebuilt the church, later understanding them as a call to renew the whole Church.
