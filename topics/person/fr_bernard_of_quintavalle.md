description: "Brother Bernard of Quintavalle"
---

Bernard of Quintavalle (died c. 1241) was a wealthy citizen of Assisi and the first man to join Francis. After giving all his goods to the poor in 1208, he became the senior brother of the new fraternity, and Francis held him in special affection.
