description: "Saint Dominic"
---

Saint Dominic (c. 1170–1221) was a Castilian priest who founded the Order of Preachers (the Dominicans). A contemporary of Francis, he shared the ideal of preaching in poverty; tradition tells of a meeting between the two founders in Rome.
