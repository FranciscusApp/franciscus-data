description: "Lady Jacopa dei Settesoli"
---

Lady Jacopa dei Settesoli (c. 1190–1273) was a Roman noblewoman and devoted friend of Francis, whom he affectionately called ‘Brother Jacopa.’ She hurried to his side at his death, bringing the almond sweets he had longed for, and was later buried near him in Assisi.
