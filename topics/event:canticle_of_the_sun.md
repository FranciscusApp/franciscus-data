The Canticle of the Sun is a hymn of praise that Francis composed around 1225, near the end of his life, blessing God through ‘Brother Sun,’ ‘Sister Moon,’ and all creatures. One of the earliest poems in the Italian vernacular, it expresses the heart of his vision of creation.
