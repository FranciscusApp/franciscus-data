Lady Pica de Bourlemont (died c. 1210) was the mother of Saint Francis and wife of the merchant Pietro di Bernardone. Tradition remembers her as gentle and devout; she is said to have had Francis baptised with the name Giovanni while his father was away on business. Little else of her life is recorded with certainty.
