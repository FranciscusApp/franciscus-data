---
description: "Portiuncula"
---

The Portiuncula is a small chapel in the plain below Assisi, dedicated to Saint Mary of the Angels. Francis restored it with his own hands and made it the cradle of his order; here Clare received the religious habit, and here Francis died in 1226.
