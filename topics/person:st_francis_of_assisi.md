Saint Francis of Assisi (1181/82–1226) was an Italian friar who founded the Order of Friars Minor. Born to a wealthy cloth merchant, he renounced his inheritance after a profound conversion and embraced a life of poverty, preaching, and care for the poor. His devotion to the poor Christ, his love of all creation, and the stigmata he received made him one of the most venerated saints in Christian history. He is the central figure of the texts gathered in this portal.
