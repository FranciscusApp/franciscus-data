## Machine contributions

Parts of this corpus were generated by AI tools rather than a human contributor,
and are disclosed here for transparency (see also the AI note in the
[README](README.md#-a-note-on-ai)). Machine output carries `by_type: ai` in the
annotation files and is, by default, **unverified** pending human review.

- **Claude** (Anthropic) — Italian machine translations of all works (except the
  official OFM translation of the *Testamentum*), and the draft semantic
  annotations and topic pages.

Machine-generated content is not subject to copyright dedication; it is included
in the corpus under the same CC0-1.0 terms as everything else.

**Known limitations**: currently all english and italian translations are machine generated.
Contrarily to annotations, there is currently no way under the format specifications to 
know which parts of a translation are human authored or reviewed. This problem is a critical
subject once contributions start coming in and we have mixed human-AI translations.
